Who We Are

Where Excellence is Nurtured

St. Paul's Victorious Training College is a registered ICT training centre located in the heart of Kisumu, Kenya. We were founded on a simple belief: that every Kenyan deserves access to practical, industry-relevant digital skills — regardless of background or budget.

We sit in one of Kisumu's most accessible locations, surrounded by the very community we serve. Our students are school leavers, office workers, parents, and entrepreneurs — people who want to grow their digital capabilities and unlock new opportunities.

From our ICT Packages foundation programme — covering everything from computer basics to Microsoft Office and PC maintenance — to our new Frontend Web Development Foundation course, we are building the technical skills the Kenyan economy needs.

Every graduate leaves with a certificate they earned through genuine practical work, skills that function in the real world, and the confidence that comes from truly understanding technology.

"Our mission is not to produce people who passed a test. It is to produce people who can sit at any computer in Kenya and do real work."

— ST. PVTC Director

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Everything we do at ST. PVTC flows from four principles.

Practical First

Theory without practice is wasted time. Every lesson at ST. PVTC is done on a real computer, solving real problems.

Student-Centred

Small classes mean personal attention. We know every student by name and we make sure no one falls behind.

Integrity

Our certificates mean something because we never award them without genuine competence. Every grade is earned.

Continuous Growth

Technology evolves and so do we. Our newest programme — Frontend Web Development — is proof that ST. PVTC never stands still.

Community

We are part of Kisumu. Our students come from this community and our success is measured by what they achieve within it.

Excellence

"Where Excellence is Nurtured" is not just our tagline — it is the standard we hold every class, every assessment, and every certificate to.
