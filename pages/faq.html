Admissions

Anyone can apply to our ICT Packages programme — there are no academic prerequisites. We welcome school leavers, working professionals, parents, and anyone who wants to improve their digital skills. For the Frontend Web Development Foundation, applicants must have completed the ICT Packages programme or demonstrate equivalent basic computer skills.
Not at all for the ICT Packages programme. We have trained students who had never touched a computer before. Our first lessons cover everything from how to hold a mouse to how to organise files on a computer. You will not be left behind.

Courses & Learning

The ICT Packages Certificate takes 3 to 6 months depending on whether you study full-time or part-time. The Frontend Web Development Foundation is a 3-month programme. Both are available as full-time or part-time options.
The ICT Packages programme is a digital literacy foundation course covering Microsoft Office, internet skills, PC maintenance and more. It is for anyone who needs to be confident with computers. The Frontend Web Development Foundation is a technical software development course where you learn to build websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Git. It is designed for students who want to pursue a career in tech or prepare for a computer science degree.
Yes — every lesson at ST. PVTC is practical and hands-on. There is no lecture-only learning. You will be at a computer from your very first class.
By the end of the programme you will be able to build fully responsive websites from scratch, write JavaScript to add interactivity, use Git and GitHub for version control, and deploy a live website on the internet. You will also leave with a personal portfolio website that you can show to universities and employers.

Certificate & Recognition

Yes. ST. PVTC is a duly registered training institution and our ICT Packages certificate is accepted by employers and higher education institutions across Kenya. Many employers specifically require this kind of computer literacy certificate during the hiring process.
The Frontend Web Development Foundation certificate demonstrates genuine technical skills and shows universities that you are serious about computer science. While it is not a direct university entry qualification, having a real portfolio of live websites you built is a powerful advantage in any CS or IT interview or application process.
